Project detail

The Dice Roller Game is a simple yet engaging program that simulates the rolling of two six-sided dice. Each roll generates two random numbers between 1 and 6, corresponding to the faces of the dice. The game displays two images representing the outcomes of the dice rolls. The player with the higher number wins the round. If both numbers are equal, it's a tie.
